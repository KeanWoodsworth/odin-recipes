Cool Cucumber Smoothie

Description

This simple beverage is perfect for hot days! It's quick, hydrating, and delightfully toothsome. The low-acid fruits and oat milk give it a mellow base, which is complimented by the sweet jam and tangy yogurt.

More than the sum of its parts, this smoothie is filling and refreshing!

Ingredients

  • (1) 6" cucumber
  • (1½ cups) frozen mango chunks
  • (½ cup) plain yogurt
  • (1 tbsp) strawberry jam
  • (1½ cups) oat milk

Steps

  1. Throw everything into a blender, run until smooth.
  2. Pour in a tall glass, serve with a straw.
  3. That's it, enjoy!
